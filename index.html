Frequently Asked Questions

Here Are Some of the Most Common Questions Our Clients Ask

1. What types of businesses do you work with?

We primarily serve small and medium-sized enterprises (SMEs) across various industries. Whether you're a startup, family-run business, or an established company, our services are tailored to meet your unique financial needs.

2. Why is bookkeeping important for my business?

Bookkeeping ensures that your financial records are accurate, up-to-date, and organized. It helps you track expenses, manage cash flow, prepare for tax season, and make informed business decisions.

3. What is the difference between financial statements and management accounts?

Management accounts are internal reports used for decision-making on a monthly or quarterly basis. Financial statements are formal documents prepared annually for tax reporting, audits, and stakeholders.

4. How often should I reconcile my bank accounts?

We recommend reconciling bank and petty cash accounts monthly. This ensures accurate records, prevents fraud, and helps identify discrepancies early.
